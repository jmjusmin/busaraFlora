ABOUT

Well hello there! I’m Tanya, the owner and lead floral designer at BUSARA. We specialize in Upscale Fine Art floral design service. Our studio is based in Washington state, however, we travel worldwide to create the most stunning flower designs for your special events.

Our floral design approaches are organic, whimsical, and uniquely tailored to client’s preferences. We offer full floral design wedding service, private event, corporate event, residential home decor, brand promotion and advertising, and more! Our main goal is to create the most memorable floral experience for you and your event.
