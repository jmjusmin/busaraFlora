Floral design workshops

A step up from the basic floral design workshops, the classes in our Essential Arranging Series uses the most exquisite flowers and the choicest materials to create stunning, eye-catching arrangements. We will explore the floral philosophy of form, color and texture and utilize a multitude of design techniques.You will learn how to select your own flowers, accent materials and containers to achieve your desired effect and how to condition your flowers so that their beauty can last.

All classes are designed for beginners and no flower experience is necessary. These classes are not part of the Professional Training or Advanced Design Programs. There are no refunds, no date changes and no makeup classes. If you cannot attend you may send someone in your place if you notify us in advance, or you may pick up your flowers within three days of the class.

PRICE

An individual class in the series is $115; the discounted price for taking all three classes in the Essential Arranging Series is $275. Price includes the most sought-after flowers and all materials; take home everything you create.

TIME

We are working on your schedule. All classes are held from 10:00am to 3:00pm.
